DO YOU KNOW WHAT MATERIALS CAN BE RECYCLED?

The materials that can be recycled are glass, aluminum, plastic water bottles, metal scrap, different kinds of paper, electronics -computers,cellular phones, keyboards, batteries and other small electronic equipment, textile, wood, wire, cables, plastic product, rubber, etc.

HOW DOES RECYCLING PAPER HELP THE ENVIRONMENT?

RECYCLING PAPER HELPS TO REDUCE GREENHOUSE GAS EMISSIONS THAT CAN CONTRIBUTE TO CLIMATE CHANGE. IT TAKES 70% LESS ENERGY AND WATER TO RECYCLE PAPER THAN TO CREATE NEW PAPER FROM TREES.
